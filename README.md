# Reading Materials

A curated collection of research papers, books, and resources on aquaculture, tilapia genetics, and related topics.

## Topics Covered

- Tilapia genetics and genetic improvement (GIFT)
- Aquaculture health and disease management
- Fish nutrition and feeding management
- Non-lethal sampling techniques
- Genomics and SNP markers in fish breeding

## How to Use

Browse by topic folder or search by keyword. Papers prefixed with author names or dates follow standard citation conventions. The `References/` folder contains supporting bibliography files.

## Contents

Papers are organized by topic area. Key folders include:

- `Books/` — textbooks and extended references
- `GIFT paper/` — resources on Genetically Improved Farmed Tilapia
- `TiLV journal/` — Tilapia Lake Virus research
- `New reading material/` — recent additions
- `References/` — citation and bibliography materials
